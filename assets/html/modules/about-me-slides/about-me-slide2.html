During my first university year I sought out oportunities in the international field to enhance my scholastic learning. I began to reach out for public service by helping to host international visitors.

I was actively involved in speaking opportunities, student International Relations Clubs, conferences, NGO experiences, all of which directly enhanced my studies.

The first steps were, of course, challenging. My first public speech was particularly so, but as Churchill said: “Never give in — never, never, never, never, in nothing great or small, large or petty, never give in except to convictions of honor and good sense.”

I was gratified to find that it went well.
