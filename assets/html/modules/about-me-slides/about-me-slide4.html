I received numerous certificates and awards for my leadership roles in public diplomacy. The ones I am particularly proud of are a certificate from the Head of the EU delegation, Jose Manuel Pinto Teixeira, for contributions to bringing Ukraine closer to the EU.

There was also a Governmental Award from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Ukraine with a certificate recording active participation in the youth diplomatic movement and the spreading of knowledge about Ukrainian foreign policy.

In 2013, I was very fortunate to win the Cambridge Commonwealth and International Trust Scholarship against great competition.

This enabled me to study at the University of Cambridge and successfully graduate with an MPhil Degree in Environmental Policy.
